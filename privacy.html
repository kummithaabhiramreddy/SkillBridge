Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

At SkillBridge, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, such as your name, email address, location, and skills you offer.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to facilitate skill exchanges, build trust within the community, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Profile Visibility

By default, your name, bio, and skills are visible to other members of SkillBridge. You can manage your privacy settings in your dashboard.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@skillbridge.example.
